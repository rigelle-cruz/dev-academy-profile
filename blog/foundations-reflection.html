Reflection

What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

After coming across the core learning, the first thing I learned about myself is that I am very perseverant and independent. The second thing I learned about myself is that sometimes I tend to be very critical of myself, which leads to stress when doing work. Through researching emotional intelligence I learned more ways I can cultivate an understanding of myself to navigate through social complexities. Lastly, I learned that even though I am perseverant, I tend to keep working until I complete my work. As a result, I sometimes forget about my well-being. Through, the core learning about the 4 dimensions of well-being, I learned that it’s important to maintain a balance between work and health. Maintaining that balance in all 4 aspects of well-being is important to have a happy life.

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

Values, empathy and self-awareness play a key role in problem-solving while learning and programming. Our values provide a framework that guides our beliefs, attitudes and behaviour. Attaining empathy and self-awareness are key traits in successfully cultivating a understanding of one’s personal strengths and weaknesses.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

Learning is not just getting the work done and doing it correctly. There are different ways to approach challenges that will build a healthier working environment.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

The most challenging part of the core learning is whenever I have to do some self-reflection in terms of my study habits. And having an open mind in adapting better habits to maintain a balanced work routine.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

Core learning is important in maintaining a healthy and productive learning environment.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practise programming instead? Justify your answer.

No, because core learning acknowledges ways we can tackle challenges without neglecting our well-being. It’s not just about doing the work, but to become successful, we also need to have an understanding of ourselves and our environment.
