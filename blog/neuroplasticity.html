Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset

What is neuroplasticity and the benefits of its principles in people?

Neuroplasticity is the brain’s ability to adapt to experiences. In which refers to the brain’s capacity to change, reorganise, or grow neural networks.

Why is Neuroplasticity is beneficial?

  • Enables people to learn new things
  • Enhances existing skills
  • Helps construct new ways of functioning, especially those with learning disabilities
  • Increases brain volume and memory
  • Helps with the recovery of strokes and other brain based injuries

How you might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your benefit

Engaging with the principles of neuroplasticity through the application of habitual learning can increase brain cognition. In other words, forming learning habits can benefit the ability to learn.

What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?

Exercising the brain through practice and repetition can increase neuroplasticity, which in turn encourages better brain function and improves memory and problem-solving skills.

What is Growth Minset and why is it relevant?

Having a growth mindset is an individual's ability to understand that their talents and abilities can be developed through various efforts and persistence. A growth mindset is relevant because it empowers an individual to believe that they can achieve anything by evolving their skills through practice and time.

In this exploration, what changed for me?

This exploration has changed the way I view myself and how I can create habits for success. I learned that possessing a growth mindset will help me gain confidence in achieving my goals and overcoming challenges, because abilities can be developed and cultivated with practice and time.

How will I integrate growth mindset into my learning journey?

To integrate a growth mindset in my learning journey, I will become aware of my abilities and strive to cultivate my skills through continuous practice. I will also work on positively changing the way I think about challenges that I come across.
